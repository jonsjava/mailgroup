Logging in:

                After you have signed-up for our Group Mail, logging in is very simple.  In the address bar in your web browser type <----INSERT SITE URL HERE---->.  In the blank to the right of the label “Username”, type in your username.  In the blank to the right of the label “Password”, type in your password.  Then select the “Submit” button to access your account.  If you are unable to get into your account make sure that your username and password are correct and retry.  If you are still unsuccessful please call Customer Support at 450-6006 for further assistance.  Back to Top

Logging Out:      

                On every screen you have to option to log out.  In the upper right side of every page there is a red button that is a log out button(shown above).  By clicking this button you will be taken back to the log in screen.  It is recommended that you log out every time that you want to leave our Group Mailer, rather than just closing the page or going to another page.  Back to Top

Adding Contacts:    

                After logging in you will be taken to a page where you can compose a message to your group, at the top of this page you have three options.  “Add Contacts” is the middle option of the three, and it is denoted with the picture shown above.  Simply select this option and you will be taken to an “ADD CONTACTS” page.  In the box in the middle of the page, type in the email addresses that you would like to have in your group.  Type one email address per line, for example:

                webmaster@localhost

                webmaster@localhost

                webmaster@localhost

After you have inserted all the email address that you would like to add, simply press the “Submit” button at the bottom of the page.  Back to Top

Deleting Contacts:    

                After logging in you will be taken to a page where you can compose a message to your group, at the top of this page you have three options.  In order to delete any of your contacts you must choose he option on the right, “Show All Contacts” (shown above).  After selecting this button you will be taken to a list of the contact that are in your mailing list.  To delete a single account simply find the account in the list and select the recycling bin that is to the left of the email address.  To delete more than one account at a time simply place a checkmark in the check box (you do this by left clicking the white box) to the left of every email address that you would like to delete, then go to the bottom of the page an select delete.   You can delete both verified and unverified accounts using these two methods.  Back to Top

 

Verified Accounts:

                Verified accounts are the email addresses that you have added to your contacts who have accepted your invitation to your group email.  Our Group Mail will only allow you to send email to email addresses who have accepted an invitation to join your group (as outlined in our Terms of Service).  This serves two purposes, it helps protect email users from getting unwanted bulk messages, and it also protects our mail from being deemed spam.  Back to Top

Unverified Accounts:

                Unverified accounts are the email addresses that you have added to you contacts who have not accepted the invitation to your group email.  Our Group Mail will only allow you to send email to email addresses who have accepted an invitation to join your group (as outlined in our Terms of Service).  These contacts may not have checked their email to know that they have been invited to a group, or they may not have wanted to be a part of the group.  Back to Top

Sending Email:

                After logging in, you will be taken to a page where you can compose a message to your group.  To send a message to all of your contacts, simply type a message in the box provided and select “Send”.  You messages will be delivered to all of your contacts.  Back to Top

Terms of Service:
